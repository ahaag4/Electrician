Electrical Services FAQs

How quickly can you respond to emergency calls?
Our emergency team responds within 30 minutes for urgent electrical issues in metro areas. For life-threatening situations like electrical fires or shocks, we prioritize immediate response. We're available 24/7 for all electrical emergencies.
What is included in your electrical wiring service?
Our complete wiring service includes: Site inspection and planning, high-quality wires and conduits, switchboard installation, proper earthing and grounding, circuit breaker setup, lighting point installation, testing and certification, and 2-year warranty on all wiring work.
How much does EV charger installation cost?
EV charger installation starts from ₹15,000 for basic home setups. The cost varies based on: Charger type (AC/DC), power requirements (3.3kW to 22kW), wiring distance from meter, load enhancement requirements, and any additional safety features needed. We provide free site inspection and detailed quotes.
Are your electricians certified and insured?
Yes, all our electricians are certified with government-approved training and carry valid licenses. We are fully insured with comprehensive liability coverage that protects both our workers and your property during work. We also provide work completion certificates for all major installations.
Do you provide free estimates?
Yes, we provide free detailed estimates after site inspection. Our estimates include: Labor costs, material specifications, timeline, warranty details, and any necessary permits. No hidden charges - what you see in the estimate is what you pay.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all payment methods: Cash, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cheques. For large projects, we typically require 30% advance for material procurement, 40% during work, and 30% upon completion and satisfaction.
How long does house wiring take?
For a standard 2BHK apartment: 3-5 days. 3BHK house: 5-7 days. Larger villas or commercial spaces: 7-14 days depending on complexity. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase and work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine.
